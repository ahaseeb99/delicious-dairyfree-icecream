FAQ

Sort

General Products Orders Company

General

  • Q: Are your ice creams really dairy free?

    A: Yes! Every flavor is 100% dairy-free. We started this journey because our family went dairy-free, so we make sure all our ice creams are safe and satisfying.

  • Q: Are your products allergen-friendly?

    A: We use dairy-free ingredients, but some flavors may contain nuts, cookies, or other allergens. Please check the ingredient list on each flavor before enjoying.

  • Q: Do your ice creams taste like regular ice cream?

    A: Absolutely — creamy, rich, and indulgent. You won’t miss the dairy.

  • Q: Do you only sell ice cream?

    A: Right now, yes! We’re focused on making the best dairy-free ice cream possible, with fun and nostalgic flavors.

Products

  • Q: What flavors do you offer?

    A: Our lineup includes classics and creative twists: Butta Pēcan, Pistachios, Peach Cobbla, Nana Puddin, Suga In Ya Tank, Birthday Cake, Cookies & Cream, Mint, Mint Cookies & Cream, Chocolate Mess, Chocolate, and Matcha.

  • Q: Do you use artificial flavors or colors?

    A: Nope! We focus on real, simple ingredients whenever possible — no unnecessary fillers.

  • Q: How do you make it creamy without dairy?

    A: Our base is made from carefully chosen plant-based ingredients that give you that smooth, creamy texture.

  • Q: Are your ice creams gluten-free?

    A: Some are, some aren’t. Flavors with cookies, cake, or cobbler may contain gluten. Always check the flavor details before ordering.

Orders

  • Q: Where can I buy your ice cream?

    A: You can order directly through our website. We’re also working to get into local stores — stay tuned!

  • Q: Do you ship nationwide?

    A: Yes! We pack our ice creams in insulated containers with dry ice to keep them frozen on the way to your door.

  • Q: How long will shipping take?

    A: Orders are typically processed within 1–2 business days. Delivery time depends on your location, but most arrive within 2–5 days.

  • Q: What if my ice cream arrives melted?

    A: That shouldn’t happen — but if it does, reach out to us right away and we’ll make it right.

Company

  • Q: How did this brand get started?

    A: Our founder’s family went dairy-free but couldn’t find enough fun, affordable flavors. So, we started making our own!

  • Q: What makes your ice cream different?

    A: We’re not just dairy-free — we’re flavor-obsessed. From Peach Cobbla to Suga In Ya Tank, we’re bringing joy back to dairy-free desserts.

  • Q: Do you have a physical shop?

    A: Not yet — but we’re planning pop-ups and events soon. Keep an eye on our social pages for updates.

  • Q: How can I stay updated?

    A: Follow us on social media or sign up for our newsletter for flavor drops, special offers, and behind-the-scenes fun.
