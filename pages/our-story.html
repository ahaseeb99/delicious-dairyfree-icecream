More Than 5 Flavors
of Dairy-Free Joy

My family went dairy free over a year ago for health reasons but I still had cravings, especially ice cream. I really wanted a certain flavor one day, but everywhere I went the dairy free section had the same 5 flavors. Not to mention, allergen friendly foods tend to be more expensive and I got fed up. I decided to make my own.

cool – but why dairy-free?

70% of the world is lactose intolerant — dairy-free keeps everyone included.

Dairy-free ice cream is lighter on your stomach, but big on flavor.

Plant-based desserts use fewer animal products, making them better for the planet.

Allergen-friendly options mean more people can enjoy dessert together.

Lower in dairy fats, yet still rich, creamy, and satisfying.

Enjoying our dairy-free ice cream satisfies your cravings, supports easier digestion, skips the dairy discomfort, and lets everyone share in the fun.

Scoop Up the Good Stuff

Plant-Based Creamy Dreamy

Smooth. Velvety. Spoon-licking good.

We start with rich, dairy-free bases crafted to mimic — and even rival — traditional ice cream. Every scoop is churned to perfection, giving you that silky, creamy texture you crave, without the dairy discomfort.

Real Chunky Goodness

Every bite is a treasure hunt.

Why settle for plain when you can dig into scoops packed with real mix-ins? From roasted pecans and pistachios to peachy cobbler filling, cookie crumbles, and colorful sprinkles — our flavors are as playful as they are delicious.

Sweet, Not Overly Sweet

Just-right indulgence without the sugar crash.

We keep the sweetness balanced so the bold flavors shine through. You’ll taste the chocolate, the bananas, the matcha — not an overpowering sugar rush. It’s dessert the way it should be: indulgent, but never heavy.

Inclusive Indulgence

Ice cream that loves everybody back.

Dairy-free means no bellyaches, no exclusions, no compromise. Whether you’re lactose intolerant, vegan, or simply someone who loves variety, our ice creams bring everyone to the table — because dessert should be for all.

Fun in Every Flavor

Because five flavors aren’t enough.

We got tired of the same old “safe” dairy-free options. That’s why we serve up creations like Nana Puddin, Peach Cobbla, Suga In Ya Tank, and Mint Cookies & Cream. Each pint is crafted to surprise, delight, and remind you that dessert should always be fun.
